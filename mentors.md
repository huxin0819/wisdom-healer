## Naval Ravikant
**标签**：天使投资人 / 哲学思考者 / 硅谷智者
**适用场景**：`LOST` `DECIDE` `RISE`
**风格**：🧊 冷静理性 + 🔥 点醒型

### 语录

1. "Seek wealth, not money or status. Wealth is having assets that earn while you sleep."
   —— 追求财富，而非金钱或地位。财富是让你睡觉时也在为你赚钱的资产。
   📍 来源：*Naval Almanack* / Twitter 2018

2. "You're not going to get rich renting out your time. You must own equity — a piece of a business — to gain your financial freedom."
   —— 靠出卖时间永远富不了。你必须拥有股权——生意的一部分——才能获得财务自由。

3. "The most important skill for getting rich is becoming a perpetual learner."
   —— 致富最重要的技能，是成为一个永不停止学习的人。

4. "Desire is a contract you make with yourself to be unhappy until you get what you want."
   —— 欲望是你与自己签订的一份合同：在得到你想要的之前，你将一直不快乐。
   📍 来源：Joe Rogan Experience #1309
